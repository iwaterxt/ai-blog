3:Td17,<h1>Softmax 优化器：从 softmax 到归一化梯度</h1>
<h2>从向量到矩阵</h2>
<p>在深度学习中，优化器的本质是梯度下降。在参数空间 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="%5CTheta">\Theta</span> 中，我们希望找到：</p>
<p><span class="katex-display" data-math="%5Ctheta%5E*%20%3D%20%5Carg%5Cmin_%7B%5Ctheta%7D%20%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D(%5Ctheta)"><br/>\theta^* = \arg\min_{\theta} \mathcal{L}(\theta)<br/></span></p>
<p>标准 SGD 的更新规则为：</p>
<p><span class="katex-display" data-math="%5Ctheta_%7Bt%2B1%7D%20%3D%20%5Ctheta_t%20-%20%5Ceta_t%20%5Cnabla%20%5Cmathcal%7BL%7D(%5Ctheta_t)"><br/>\theta_{t+1} = \theta_t - \eta_t \nabla \mathcal{L}(\theta_t)<br/></span></p>
<p>但对于矩阵参数（如线性层的权重 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="W%20%5Cin%20%5Cmathbb%7BR%7D%5E%7Bm%20%5Ctimes%20n%7D">W \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}</span>），简单地按坐标下降可能不是最有效的方式。</p>
<h2>msign 函数</h2>
<p>Muon 优化器的核心运算是 <strong>矩阵符号函数</strong> msign。对于对称矩阵 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="M%20%3D%20G%20G%5ET">M = G G^T</span>，有：</p>
<p><span class="katex-display" data-math="M%20%3D%20U%20%5CSigma%20U%5ET%2C%20%5Cquad%20%5Ctext%7Bmsign%7D(M)%20%3D%20U%20%5Ccdot%20%5Ctext%7Bdiag%7D(%5Ctext%7Bsign%7D(%5Csigma_i))%20%5Ccdot%20U%5ET"><br/>M = U \Sigma U^T, \quad \text{msign}(M) = U \cdot \text{diag}(\text{sign}(\sigma_i)) \cdot U^T<br/></span></p>
<p>直观上，msign 找到矩阵的"主特征向量方向"，并将对应特征值投影到 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="%5Cpm%201">\pm 1</span>。</p>
<h2>Newton-Schulz 迭代</h2>
<p>直接计算 msign 需要 SVD，代价较高。Newton-Schulz 迭代提供了一种高效近似：</p>
<p><span class="katex-display" data-math="X_%7Bk%2B1%7D%20%3D%20%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%7D%20X_k%20(3I%20-%20X_k%5ET%20X_k)"><br/>X_{k+1} = \frac{1}{2} X_k (3I - X_k^T X_k)<br/></span></p>
<p>初始 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="X_0%20%3D%20G%20%2F%20%5C%7CG%5C%7C_F">X_0 = G / \|G\|_F</span>，迭代数次后 <span class="katex-inline" data-math="X_k%20%5Capprox%20%5Ctext%7Bmsign%7D(GG%5ET)">X_k \approx \text{msign}(GG^T)</span>。</p>
<h2>代码实现</h2>
<pre><code class="hljs language-python">def newton_schulz_iterate(G, num_iter=5):
    """Fast msign approximation via Newton-Schulz iteration."""
    X = G / (G.norm() + 1e-8)
    I = torch.eye(G.shape[0], device=G.device)
    for _ in range(num_iter):
        X = 0.5 <em> X @ (3 </em> I - X.T @ X)
    return X</code></pre>
<h2>稳定性分析</h2>
<p>为什么 Muon 有效？从三个稳定性指标来看：</p>
<li><strong>前向稳定性</strong>：<span class="katex-inline" data-math="%5C%7CWx%5C%7C_2%20%2F%20%5C%7Cx%5C%7C_2%20%3D%20%5CTheta(1)">\|Wx\|_2 / \|x\|_2 = \Theta(1)</span></li>
<li><strong>依赖稳定性</strong>：<span class="katex-inline" data-math="%5C%7CW(x_1%20-%20x_2)%5C%7C_2%20%2F%20%5C%7Cx_1%20-%20x_2%5C%7C_2%20%3D%20%5CTheta(1)">\|W(x_1 - x_2)\|_2 / \|x_1 - x_2\|_2 = \Theta(1)</span></li>
<li><strong>更新稳定性</strong>：<span class="katex-inline" data-math="%5C%7CW%5CDelta%5C%7C%20%2F%20%5C%7C%5CDelta%5C%7C%20%3D%20%5CTheta(1)">\|W\Delta\| / \|\Delta\| = \Theta(1)</span></li>
